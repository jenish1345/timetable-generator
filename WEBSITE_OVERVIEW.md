## 🚀 How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Open the Application
**Double-click** `START_HERE.html` or `index.html`

### Step 2: Follow the Wizard
1. Enter configuration (days, periods, subjects, teachers)
2. Add subject names
3. Add teacher names
4. Map subjects to teachers
5. Click "Generate"

### Step 3: View & Export
- See your beautiful timetable
- Click "Regenerate" for different layouts
- Click "Print" or "Export PDF" to save

**That's it! No installation, no coding, no complexity!**
